    About Malaika Comfort Care

    Welcome to Malaika Comfort Care, “We assure to love your loved ones’. Here at Malaika, you will experience an individualized treatment plan designed to enable you to achieve your maximum level of physical and cognitive function to restore your independence.

    Our Mission

    The mission of Malaika Comfort Care is to provide quality home care services. Our firm seeks to help meet the physical, mental, and emotional needs of our seniors and their families. We are dedicated to accomplishing these services in a highly professional and dignified manner, in an atmosphere most beneficial and cost-effective.


    Our Vision

    At Malaika Comfort Care, we have a new way of thinking of Seniors by Engaging hearts, transforming lives, Erasing boundaries. We want our communities to be not just places where seniors live, but places where seniors flourish.
    We aim to create communities where people are empowered and engaged, providing environments that foster independence and joy.
    Our holistic approach to care moves beyond physical care to address all of the intangible aspects of wellness—meeting the needs of the whole person and enhancing our residents’ quality of life.

    Licensure for assisted-living facilities.

    Type A- in Type A facilities. A resident must be physically and mentally capable of evacuating the facility unassisted. This may include the mobile non-ambulatory person such as those who are on wheelchairs or electric carts and have the capacity to transfer and evacuate themselves in an emergency. This resident does not require routine attendance during night-time sleeping hours and must be capa.
    Type B- in Type B facilities, a resident may require staff assistance to evacuate and may be incapable of following directions under emergency conditions. They may require attendance during night-time sleeping hours. The resident may not be permanently bedfast but may require assistance in transferring to and from on a wheelchair.
